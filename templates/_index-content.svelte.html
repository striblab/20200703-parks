Gaps in the Minneapolis park system
The Trust for Public Land determines where parks are most needed in cities across the country by analyzing where residents live, with additional consideration given to areas with more youth and low-income populations. The map below shows where they have identified park gaps in Minneapolis. The map excludes park need in non-residential areas like industrial and cemetery zones.
